Rank is based on messages sent or points when points are implemented
?rank [user] -> view rank of user or self
?rank top -> view top ranks

Log control command
?log reset [channel] -> reset channels log.json and log.txt completely.
?log confirm -> confirm above command
?log reparse [channel] -> repopulate log.json
?log clear [time ms or str] -> clear a portion of this channels messages that are not older than inputted time
?log clear channel [time ms or str] -> 2 param version of above for specifying a channel
